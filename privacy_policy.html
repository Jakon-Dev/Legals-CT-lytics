Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how CT-lytics ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your data when you access or use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and hashed password used for registration and authentication.
  • Riot Games Data (RSO): When you link your Riot Games account via Riot Sign-On, we collect your unique Player Universally Unique Identifier (PUUID), Region, and Game Name. We do not have access to your Riot Games password or login credentials.
  • Team and User Input: Names of teams, players, scrim stats, practice performance, and manual inputs via our forms.
  • Match Data: Practice and official match data processed via images, video links, or integrations (e.g., Riot Games API).
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics data such as browser type, device, region, pages visited, and time on site.
  • Cookies and Session Data: For keeping you logged in and enhancing performance and usability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage user accounts and authentications (including RSO)
  • To fetch and display your Valorant match history and stats
  • To allow creation and management of teams
  • To track and visualize scrim and match performance
  • To provide insights and reports for improvement
  • To ensure fair use and detect abuse
  • To improve the app based on aggregated user behavior

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal or team data to third parties. However, we may share anonymized aggregate data for research or performance benchmarking. In limited cases, we may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., hosting platforms, analytics tools) strictly to operate the platform.

4. Cookies

We use essential and performance cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in across sessions
  • Improve loading times and user experience
  • Measure performance through analytics

You can adjust cookie settings in your browser at any time.

5. Data Retention

We retain user and team data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your associated data within 30 days, unless retention is required by law.

6. Security

We implement technical and organizational safeguards including encryption, secure authentication, and regular audits. However, no system is 100% secure, and we encourage you to use strong passwords.
